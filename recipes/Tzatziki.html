Tzatziki recipe

Description

Simple but tasty Joghurt sauce

Ingredients

  • 500g of greek Joghurt
  • Half a grated cucumber
  • 2-4 cloves of garlic crushed
  • Salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • A drizzle of olive oil

Steps

  1. Put the joghurt in a bowl
  2. Squeeze the liquid out of the cucumber and add it to the joghurt
  3. Add the crushed garlic salt and pepper and combine the ingredients
  4. Before serving drizzle olive oil over the joghurt sauce
